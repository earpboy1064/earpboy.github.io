About Kemp Contracting

Services

Kemp Contracting is a privately owned contracting company that specializes in commercial and residential contracting. We provide a start to finish business product that specializes in metal roofing and professional gate services.

Our Story

Kemp Contracting started out as a locally owned business in Garland Texas. We specialized in residential needs that included multiple specializations including retaining walls, metal roofing and electrical gates. We have grown into a commercial business that can create a custom experience using metal roofing and metal buildings.
